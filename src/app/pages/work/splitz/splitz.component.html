Splitz

Project Description

In my quest for a more organized browsing experience, I found myself frustrated with the clutter of disorganized, open tabs in Google Chrome. Unable to find a suitable solution, I decided to take matters into my own hands and created Splitz, a Chrome extension designed to help users manage tabs efficiently and have a better experience browsing the Internet. Creating Splitz wasn't just about tidying up my tabs, however; it was also about my commitment to continual learning and professional growth. Independently exploring new technologies and expanding my skill set, I poured my enthusiasm into crafting a tool that uncovered a whole new world of possibilities in web development. As my first personal project, this experience fundamentally changed how I viewed software engineering, making me realize the endless potential that lies in creating things that seamlessly work together on the web. The eventual, successful publication of Splitz on the Chrome Web Store marks not just a project but a milestone in my journey as a software engineer, reflecting my dedication to innovation and problem-solving.

Features

  • Create categories to organize your tabs
  • Drag and drop tabs to the desired category
  • Quickly switch between categories using keyboard shortcuts
  • Add and remove tabs
  • Light & Dark Modes
