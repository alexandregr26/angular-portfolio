PROJECT DESCRIPTION

In my quest for a more organized browsing experience, I found myself frustrated with the clutter of disorganized, open tabs in Google Chrome. Unable to find a suitable solution, I decided to take matters into my own hands and created Splitz, a Chrome extension designed to help users manage tabs efficiently and have a better experience browsing the Internet.

Creating Splitz wasn't just about tidying up my tabs, however; it was also about my commitment to continual learning and professional growth. As my first personal project, this experience fundamentally changed how I viewed software engineering, making me realize the endless potential that lies in creating things that seamlessly work together on the web.

Why I Built Splitz

I built Splitz to solve a problem I faced every day: too many open tabs and no good way to organize them.

This project taught me how powerful small tools can be when they solve real problems. It was my first personal project, and publishing it on the Chrome Web Store was a major milestone in my journey as a developer.
