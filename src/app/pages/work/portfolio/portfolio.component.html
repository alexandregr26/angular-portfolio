Project Description

Motivated by a profound passion for learning and driven to carve out a unique and authentic professional identity, I designed this online portfolio. Initially developed in 2020 using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I rebuilt it this year with Angular, adopting a more structured and modern approach to upgrade the user experience. In the ever-evolving field of software engineering, I firmly believe in and follow the necessity for continual learning and adapting to newer technologies.

This portfolio is not just a digital showcase of my work; it encapsulates my commitment to breaking free from the standard resume conventions and embracing innovation in narrating my professional story - it is my way of giving recruiters and future employers a better look at my journey from an electric and computer engineering student to the software engineer I am today.
