Position Summary

As a Software Developer Intern at Thomson Reuters, I contributed to developing web applications using modern technologies such as AWS, C#, .NET, and Angular. My role primarily focused on building and revamping REST APIs, ensuring they adhered to industry best practices. This effort enhanced usability and performance across various services and laid the foundation for further work in backend development.

As I advanced in this area, I took on the challenge of automating onboarding processes, optimizing workflows, implementing secure token exchange systems, and managing user sessions, which deepened my understanding of modern authorization protocols like OAuth and best practices in identity and access management. However, my responsibilities did not stop at the backend; I was also involved in designing and developing responsive, user-friendly interfaces that ensured a seamless experience for end-users. This hands-on work in full-stack development equipped me with a well-rounded technical knowledge, which I continue to build upon.

Beyond technical contributions, I actively participated in Agile development processes. I frequently led daily stand-up meetings, sprint planning sessions, and retrospective discussions, strengthening my teamwork and project management skills. Reflecting on this internship, I realize it not only refined my software engineering skills but also improved my ability to collaborate effectively in a dynamic, international setting.
